Membuat Jaringan VLAN 100, 200, 300 dan 400 di 3 Lantai Menggunakan 3 Switch
Router yang digunakan : Router Cisco 3640

1. Setting Router
   VLAN 100
   R1>enable
   R1#configure terminal
   R1(config)#interface Ethernet 0/0
   R1(config-if)#interface Ethernet 0/0.58 
   R1(config-subif)#encapsulation dot1q 100
   R1(config-subif)#ip address 192.143.58.1 255.255.255.0
   R1(config-subif)#end
   R1(config-if)#exit
   R1(config)#exit
   %SYS-5-CONFIG_I: Configured from console by console
